BURP B R A N D
F O U N D A T I O N S
A room to feast on Scripture — and to
honestly say what you found.
A faith-centered community for Christians who love God's Word, study it
deeply, and gather to reflect honestly. We feast on Scripture daily, then come
together to "burp" — sharing what stood out, what challenged us, what stirred
us, what confused us.
BRAND PILLARS
Feast. Scripture as nourishment — slow, daily, satisfying.
Reflect. The "burp" — honest, humble, sometimes unresolved.
Question. Bereans verified what they were taught. We do too.
Gather. Upper Room — small rooms, real people, presence over performance.
Grow. Long arc. Old-world weight. Not built for the algorithm.

HARD RULES
Three fonts only — Cormorant Garamond, Inter, Cinzel.
Four colors only — Parchment, Ink, Stone, Gold. Gold is scarce.
Wide tracking (0.18em–0.32em) on serif caps. Always.
Dot-rule is the signature divider. Never a button or card border.
Sentence case for headings and buttons. ALL CAPS only on the wordmark and Cinzel eyebrows.
No emoji in product UI. No staged stock photography. No spring/bounce easing.

BURP Web — UI kit
An interactive recreation of the platform — feed, scripture reader, reflection
composer, and Upper Room. See . A separate

print file ships every screen at ui_kits/burp-web/index-print.html .
SCREENS
PRIMITIVES (PRIMITIVES.JSX)
Home feed — the burp wall
Scripture reader — long-form passage view Button, Tag, Avatar, AvatarStack
Reflection composer — modal, gold focus ring Field (input + textarea)
Upper Room — a single small group DotRule, Eyebrow, Icon
HOW TO USE THIS SYSTEM
1. Link colors_and_type.css at the top of any page. Tokens are CSS custom properties — use them,
do not override.
2. Pull in fonts via Google Fonts (Inter) and the bundled TTFs (Cormorant Garamond, Cinzel).
3. Compose UI by reaching for the primitives in . Lift them into prototypes as-is.
ui_kits/burp-web/
4. Read the Voice page before writing any new copy. Tone drift is the most common failure mode.
CAVEATS & OPEN QUESTIONS
Cormorant Garamond not supplied — EB Garamond bundled behind the alias.
Inter pulled from Google Fonts at runtime.
Phosphor Regular substituted as the icon set.
No codebase or Figma supplied — the UI kit is a brand-DNA recreation, not a port.
